In part 1, you will work on a task that consists of a sequence of arithmetic questions. You will try to answer as many arithmetic problems as possible in {{ gameDuration }}. Each arithmetic problem requires you to add three numbers.

Example Question: Three plus four plus seven
Correct answer: 14

Even though the question expressed numbers in a written form, you must enter your answers in numeric form, for example `14’ rather than ‘fourteen’. You will not be able to click the submit button before you answer each question. If you want to proceed to the next question without necessarily getting the answer right, you can type `1’ and click submit.

There is no penalty for getting answers wrong. Your goal is to answer as many questions correctly as possible.

You will be paid based on the number of questions you answer correctly in a period of {{ gameDuration }}. You will earn 5 cents for each correct answer.

At the end of the {{ gameDuration }}, the program will display the number of questions you attempted and the number of questions you got right.

Good luck!
